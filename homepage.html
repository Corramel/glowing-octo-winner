What ifzombies existed?

Well,"zombies" wouldn't really be undead at all. They would most likely be humans with pumped up hormones, either from chemicals, or some sort of mutation. Though, first thing's first, we have to talk about the spread of this so called "virus".


These world-wide soft drink companies have decided to design a new drink. One to improve intelligence, become more ambitious, stay healthier, and keep you motivated... All for the wrong reasons. By adding new substances and hormones into their formula, they cause hightened aggression, more stress, addiction, and mood changes, lack of sleep, and even more side effects. The effects will vary from individual to individual, but as people begin to drink this new and improved formula, it ends up increasing the effects, and making them crave more drinks...
